Celebrating Hawaiian Culture

E Ulu.
Fest.

Food. Music. Culture. Community.

Join us for a one-day festival celebrating the culture, food, music, and people of Hawaiʻi. Enjoy live entertainment, delicious local food, and a marketplace featuring Native Hawaiian-owned businesses offering apparel, jewelry, artwork, and other unique products. Throughout the day, you'll have the opportunity to participate in hands-on cultural activities such as lei making, poi pounding, and traditional fish net throwing, while learning about Hawaiian traditions and connecting with others in the community. Most importantly, proceeds from the event will support initiatives that give back to Hawaiʻi and help strengthen Native Hawaiian communities. Whether you're reconnecting with your roots, curious to learn more about Hawaiian culture, or simply looking for a fun day with family and friends, everyone is welcome to join us in celebrating the spirit of aloha.

Who We Are

More than a festival.

E Ulu Fest is a celebration of the culture, food, and people of Hawaiʻi. We bring together Hawaiian-owned businesses, local food vendors, musicians, and cultural practitioners to create a day that's as fun as it is meaningful. Come for the vibes, leave with a deeper connection to the spirit of aloha.
